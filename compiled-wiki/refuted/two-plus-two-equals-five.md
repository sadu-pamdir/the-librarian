# Refuted: "2 + 2 = 5"

Within Peano arithmetic, addition is defined recursively and the successor
function is injective. From these axioms, 2 + 2 = 4 is a theorem, and
2 + 2 = 5 is provably false — not unlikely, not disputed: **false within the
system that defines the claim**.

refuted by proof. It anchors the scale — everything else in this wing must
meet an evidence standard proportionate to its claim type.
